#+title: Generative Adversarial Networks
#+filetags: :literature:

GAN

Network composed of two adversarial networks. Both of them can be MLP or CNN:
- Generator
- Discriminator to distinguish between real and fake results.

The purpose is to have a discriminator unable to distinguish between real and fake results, by training.
