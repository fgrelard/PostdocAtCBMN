#+title: Infrared microscopy
[[id:b0a93f17-8579-40eb-9ad9-2f6e5daad578][Infrared spectroscopy]]

Imaging with infrared spectroscopy.

Infrared measurements on different points of the sample. The stage is moving to get a spectre at different pixel positions.

The measurement is done on a region of interest on the sample.

_Analysis_:
- Univariate analysis
- [[id:74e38001-568b-42ec-a8f2-bb8a4f39013a][Multivariate analysis]]: chemometrics

_Processing_:
1. Preprocessing: [[id:086dbae6-8f19-47b8-9543-782e16adcd00][inverse problems]], baseline subtraction, smoothing
2. Exploration: [[id:71035313-ca28-4397-8084-15dc5840e0c7][PCA]]
3. Resolution/regression: PLS-DA
4. Segmentation: kNN, PLS-DA

We get image and spectral signatures that are easier to interpret.
